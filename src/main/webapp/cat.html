General Behavior

Cats are normally a nice addition to a make your life just a little more exciting. Cats are chilled and relax creatures, who lay where they want, and interact with you when they want to. They are quite independent and some are very quiet.

Food

It is important to feed your cat three times a day, about 3 ounces, spread accross the whole day. Cat normally would tell you if they are hungry by surrounding their food bowl, or getting your attention and leading you over to their bowl.

Playing with your cat

Cats are normally chilled and lazy animals, but it important to play with them, whether it a ranged toy or a throwable one, it helps cat burn off calories and energy.
